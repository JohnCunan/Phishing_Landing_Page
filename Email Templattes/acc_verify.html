As part of our ongoing commitment to safeguarding sensitive information and enhancing cybersecurity measures, we will be transitioning from traditional password-based authentication to Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) using Microsft Authenticator for all Microsoft email accounts.

Two-Factor Authentication adds an extra layer of security by requiring not only a password but also a second form of verification, typically a unique code sent to a trusted device. This significantly reduces the risk of unauthorized access, phishing attacks, and other potential security threats.

This change will take affect on May 4, 2024 UTC

You are kindly requested to re-register your account with Microsoft Authenticator before the effective date, failing which your account will be subject to lockout.
